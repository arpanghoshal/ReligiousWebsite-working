    About Us

    The Bengalee Association was founded and registered on 16th October 1999. The Association organises and participates in various activities throughout the year in keeping with the objectives set out in our constitution.

    Apart from all major celebrations for the Bengalis throughout the year, our Association is closely engaged in many philanthropic activities. Our Durga Puja celebration, for example, is one of the largests in the country attracting close to a 10000 visitors in the four days of festivities. Our Puja is not just a religious event, it is a socio-cultural activity for thousands of people. Locals too visit us in large numbers during the puja days. We serve free Bhog Prasad through out the four days. The other puja's celebrated are Lakshmi Puja, Kali Puja and Saraswati Puja with other get-togethers and picnics.

    Durgotsav

    The most awaited festival of the year bringing smile to every face is celebrated arund 6 days from Maha Panchami to Maha Dashmi.

    Lakshmi Puja

    Five days after durga puja, lakshmi puja is celebrated. Puja is performed at the evening followed by bhog prasad.

    Kali Puja

    Goddess Kali is worshipped during Diwali, the puja is followed with bhog prasad. As the puja continues till early morning, seating arrangements are made for the devotees.

    Saraswati Puja

    Puja commences from 11:30 am onwards at basant panchmi, all the age group females are dressed in yellow sarees. THe puja is followed by bhog prasad.

    Picnic

    Frosty winters makes us lazy and difficult to socialize, we held picnics in the month of December at various places around Punjab.

    Other get-togethers

    Apart from meeting during the pujas we make sure that the network and communication flow is tightly maintained between all the people in the cultural club.
